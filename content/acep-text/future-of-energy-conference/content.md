## Powering Africa's Industrial Transformation

The Future of Energy Conference (FEC) is an annual platform to drive stakeholder collaboration towards an inclusive and sustainable energy future for Africa. It critically explores strategies to harness the untapped potential within the energy transition and the continent’s natural resources to address energy poverty and drive economic transformation.

FEC 2026 seeks to address the structural drivers of energy costs, infrastructure readiness, and industrial energy competitiveness, ensuring that Africa’s value-addition and industrialisation ambitions are practically achievable by:

### Interrogating

structural drivers of industrial energy costs

### Assessing

infrastructure readiness and technological pathways

### Evaluating

regional integration

### Examining

Financial solutions
