 Powering Africa's Industrial Transformation 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The Future of Energy Conference (FEC) is an annual platform to drive stakeholder collaboration towards an inclusive and sustainable energy future for Africa. It critically explores strategies to harness the untapped potential within the energy transition and the continent’s natural resources to address energy poverty and drive economic transformation.FEC 2026 seeks to address the structural drivers of energy costs, infrastructure readiness, and industrial energy competitiveness, ensuring that Africa’s value-addition and industrialisation ambitions are practically achievable by:  
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Interrogatingstructural drivers of industrial energy costs 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Assessinginfrastructure readiness and technological pathways 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Evaluatingregional integration 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 ExaminingFinancial solutions 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 FEC 2026 at a Glance 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Experience firsthand the significance of collaboration and innovation as we collectively pave the way for a sustainable energy future across Africa.
